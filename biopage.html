    • architecture to UX DESIGN

    • - a transition with valuable life lessons

    • I believe the right thinking is very important, no matter if it’s engineering, the humanities, or design. And my transition from architecture to UX played a major role at shaping this. Here are 3 life lessons that this transition has taught me.

  • Self Realization

    A career requires both intense passion and belief. It’s like a bike, when you got the two wheels under you, you move easier and faster, also with joy. Inspired by Steve Jobs Biography.

  • A Growth Mindset

    Humans can iterate just like how products do. Inspired from the Design of Everyday Things and the Principles, I feel an alignment of work and life by pursuing UX.

  • Radically Embrace Reality

    UX design is Architecture in the new era. Inspired by a lot of factors.

    The progress of urbanization in the last hundreds of years enabled high frequency of spatial interaction in the cities, reducing population of the countryside that were poor at enabling human interaction. Similarly, sensations and interactions are turning from cities to the Internet.

    Internet is becoming the new City, and cities are destined to be the new countryside.
    I thrive to design “architecture” for the new “City”.
